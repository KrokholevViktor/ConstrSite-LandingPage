Stages of service provision

01
Application
Leave a request on the website or call +7 (999) 999-99-99
02consultation
Over the phone, we clarify the primary data about the site, soil characteristics, and the type of future construction. We schedule a visit from a project engineer
03Cost calculation
Together with the engineer, we will meet at your site at a time convenient for you. We will calculate the layout of the pile field and calculate the exact cost for the construction of a turnkey foundation
04Signing the contract
и предоплата
Согласовываем дату выхода монтажной бригады. Подписываем договор. Вы вносите предоплату в размере 50%
05Foundation installation
We go to the site already with piles and the necessary equipment; We drive the piles, cut them to the level, install the heads or cut the piles for a grillage.
